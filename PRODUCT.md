# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Software engineers and engineering leaders evaluating or using AI coding agents. They arrive skeptical, with little time, and need to understand whether Haft can make AI-assisted engineering more governable, reviewable, and durable.

## Product Purpose

Explain what Haft is, why it matters, and how to start using it. A successful visit lets someone state the product's role in plain language, see the mechanism behind the claim, and take the next step through installation, documentation, or the source repository.

## Brand Personality

Exact, opinionated, technical, and alive. Confident without becoming academic; expressive without turning into decorative cyberpunk.

## Anti-References

- Generic AI SaaS pages built from interchangeable gradients and slogans.
- Visual effects that compete with the product claim or installation path.
- Card-grid marketing that fragments one coherent technical story.
- Static academic documentation that hides the product's practical use.

## Design Principles

- Show the mechanism, not just the promise.
- Give each viewport one clear job and one clear next action.
- Use motion to express an active substrate, never as foreground noise.
- Earn technical density through progressive disclosure.
- Keep product claims and the installation path legible at every breakpoint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve keyboard navigation and visible focus, maintain readable contrast, avoid color-only meaning, support touch and small screens, and provide a static equivalent for reduced-motion preferences or unavailable WebGL.
